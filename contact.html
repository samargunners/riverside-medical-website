Directions & Parking

From Route 295: Take Exit 47 to Riverside Drive. Turn right onto Riverside Parkway. The Meadowbrook Professional Center will be on your left.

From Route 130: Head south on US-130. Turn left onto Riverside Parkway. The office building is approximately 0.5 miles on the right.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment?

No! We welcome walk-in patients during all business hours. However, wait times may vary depending on patient volume.

What should I bring to my visit?

Please bring your insurance card, photo ID, a list of current medications, and any relevant medical records.

Do you offer telehealth visits?

Yes! Telehealth visits are available for select conditions. Please note that not all insurance plans cover telehealth services. Call us to schedule.

How long is the typical wait time?

Wait times vary based on patient volume and complexity of cases. We strive to see all patients as quickly as possible while providing quality care.

Can you prescribe medications?

Yes, Dr. Delvadia can prescribe medications as needed and provide refills for existing prescriptions.

Do you accept new patients?

Absolutely! We welcome new patients and accept most major insurance plans. Walk-ins are always welcome.
