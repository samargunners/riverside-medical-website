Our Physician

Dr. Jayesh Delvadia, MD

Board Certified in Internal Medicine

Dr. Delvadia is a board-certified Internal Medicine physician with over 15 years of experience in primary and urgent care. He completed his residency at Springfield Medical Center in Massachusetts and graduated with honors from Georgetown University School of Medicine.

Prior to founding Riverside Primary & Walk-In Care in 2015, Dr. Delvadia worked in several urgent care facilities in the tri-state area, where he developed a passion for accessible, patient-centered healthcare.

Dr. Delvadia is affiliated with both Metropolitan General Hospital and Riverside Medical Center, ensuring continuity of care for patients requiring hospitalization or specialized services.

Education: Georgetown University School of Medicine
Residency: Springfield Medical Center, MA
Hospital Affiliations: Metropolitan General, Riverside Medical Center
